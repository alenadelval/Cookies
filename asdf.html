PEANUT BUTTER COOKIES

- my familys recipe -

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 CUP BROWN SUGAR
  • 1 CUP WHITE SUGAR
  • 1 CUP BUTTER
  • 1 CUP PEANUT BUTTER
  • 2 EGGS, BEATEN
  • 2 TEASPOONS BAKING SODA
  • 1/2 TEASPOON SALT
  • 2 1/2 CUPS FLOUR

DIRECTIONS

Preheat oven to 375Â°. Mix all ingredients in a bowl with hands. Form into balls. Flatten with fork and bake for 15 minutes.

Tips and Tricks

  1. Remeber to preheat the oven! If you put the batter in before it is done preheating, the cooking times will vary and it will not be hot enough
  2. Each oven is different so cooking times may vary
  3. Beat the eggs separately from the batter
